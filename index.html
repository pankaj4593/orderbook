Profit

50% Fee Profit:
alcurex ltd collects trading fees of 0.4% on each market. (0.2% on each side : buy / sell).
50% the collected profit will be withheld by the exchange and 50% will be paid to fee share owners as dividends.

The profit calculation formula for share owners is: (profit - server costs) * 50% / No. of shares held publicly * your shares.

Calculation Example:
If a share owner has 5,000 shares and the market daily trade volume is 200BTC:
Weekly volume = 7 * 200 = 1400
Weekly server costs = 0.056 Trading fee = 0.4% = 0.004 Share holders' earnings = 50% = 0.5 Weekly total fee profit = 0.004 * 1400 - 0.056 = 5.544
Your weekly profits = 5.544 * 0.5 / 141,960 * 5000 = 0.097633
Weekly profit: 0.0976 BTC
Monthly profit: 0.4882 BTC
Yearly profit: 5.0770 BTC
*Please note this example assumes a stable daily volume of 200 BTC and weekly server costs of 0.056 BTC.

Dividend Payments

Share Dividend Payment rules:
Share dividends are paid out to share holders every Monday at 20:00 UTC.
Shares held on account and shares placed on sell orders will both collect profits.

Details

ALCUREX Fee shares can be claimed for real company shares (alcurex Oy) at a rate of 1:10 (10 virtual shares equals one real share)
Real shares have no fee profit rights, but will legally own part of the company with all rights, divided with 28,392 shares.
Real shares can not be switched back to fee shares and legal share holders need provide identification. (For registering shares on company sharebook.)
The minimum share amount for registering is 100 actual shares. (1000 virtual shares)
(Registration is free excluding possible registration fees by Finnish government. Currently there are no fees.)
We reserve the rights to make time limited offers during which fee share owners need to decide whether they want to claim their fee shares for legal company share or continue holding virtual shares indefinitely.

The share holder is responsible for any taxes or legal fees associated with the country they reside in.

Multiple exchanges - One account

- When the order is filled (partially or fully) it is updated on alcurex as well.
0% extra trading fees
